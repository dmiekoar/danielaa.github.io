Here you can find some of my side projects

Forecasting intermittent demand using machine learning

During the M5 competitions I developed a model that forecasts the sales for the upcoming 28 days for more than 40.000 time series, using a dataset provided by Walmart. This solution ranked the top 1% (28th position) and can be found here.

Machine learning applied in sports

Can machine learning be applied in sports? Of course! Here I team up with some collegues to build a model that would predict how many yards a football player would gain after receiving a handoff.

Obtaining job positions related to Data Science at Indeed

Wonder how you can use Python and Selenium to scrap data and obtain details of job ads at Indeed? Here we scrape data from a website and retrieve main information such as job title, company name, location and summary. This information is then saved in a csv file, and you can use for your analysis on the job market.

A/B Testing in Marketing Analytics

This project shows how you can use Python to analyse the results of a A/B test, which can help you decide whether a particular feature can increase the conversion rate in your business.

Efficiency score of NYC builings

Here we use data from NYC to predict the efficiency score of building with different characteristics.
